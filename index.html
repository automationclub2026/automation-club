About the club

A practical community for future automation engineers.

The club connects students who want to move beyond theory and work on real industrial automation ideas through teamwork, workshops and project-based learning.

01

Hands-on learning

Practice PLC logic, sensors, actuators, control panels, industrial protocols and simulation tools through guided activities.

Project culture

03

Career direction

Members build portfolio-ready work and gain confidence for internships, working-student roles and research ideas.

What we do

Simple structure. Serious learning.

Our focus is clear: understand industrial systems, build small prototypes, share knowledge and grow local student automation communities.

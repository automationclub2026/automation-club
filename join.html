Membership • Join the Club

Join the Industrial Automation Club.

Become part of a student-led technical community focused on industrial automation, PLC systems, robotics, digital manufacturing, smart factories and practical engineering learning.

Who can join?

Open for motivated learners.

The club is open to students, early-career engineers and technology enthusiasts who want to learn, build and collaborate in automation.

01

Students

Engineering students from electrical, mechanical, mechatronics, computer science, industrial engineering and related fields.

02

Project Builders

Students who want to build real technical projects, contribute to research ideas and improve their engineering portfolio.

03

Beginners Welcome

No advanced experience is required. Members can start from basics and grow through workshops, mentoring and teamwork.

Process

How to join

The joining process is simple and designed for students who want to actively participate.

Submit Your Interest

Share your name, university, study program and area of interest in automation.

Attend an Introduction Session

Meet the local chapter team, understand current projects and learn how the club works.

Select Your Activity Area

Choose between projects, workshops, research, documentation, events or chapter coordination.

Start Contributing

Join a project group, support chapter activities and build practical automation experience.

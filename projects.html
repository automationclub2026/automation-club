Project portfolio

Current active projects

Four focused projects for students interested in automation, digital manufacturing, process optimization and research-based engineering.

Digital Twin

Digital Twinning for Oil Refinery Operations

A research and simulation project focused on creating a digital representation of refinery process areas to understand equipment behavior, process flow, alarms and operational performance.

FocusProcess Simulation
OutputDigital Model
  • Model tanks, pumps, valves, sensors and pipelines.
  • Study process data, control logic and alarm conditions.
  • Create a visual dashboard for monitoring simulated refinery performance.
PLC Audit

PLC-Based Automation Audit in Warehouse Systems

A practical improvement project to study warehouse automation processes and identify how PLC logic, sensor placement, interlocks and operator workflow can reduce errors.

FocusError Reduction
OutputAudit Report
  • Map warehouse flow from receiving to dispatch.
  • Identify automation failure points and process mistakes.
  • Recommend PLC logic improvements, sensor checks and HMI warnings.
Smart Maintenance

Predictive Maintenance Research for Industrial Motors

A research project exploring how vibration, temperature, current and runtime data can be used to predict motor faults before breakdowns occur.

FocusCondition Monitoring
OutputPrototype Concept
  • Study overheating, imbalance and bearing wear.
  • Compare sensor-based condition data.
  • Design a simple fault-warning concept for maintenance teams.
Automation Research

Energy-Efficient Automation for Smart Manufacturing

A research-oriented project studying how automated machines, conveyors, compressors and production cells can reduce energy consumption without reducing productivity.

FocusEfficiency
OutputOptimization Study
  • Analyze energy usage in automated production.
  • Study standby modes and smart scheduling.
  • Prepare ideas for sustainable industrial automation.
